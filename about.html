Founder & Guide

Sandy Zenobia

"With meditation, holistic living, and nature as my compass, I embarked on a journey of mindful discovery and self-alignment."

My journey began with sports as therapy, until injury led me inward. Through meditation, child healing and self-love, I reconnected with my essence, embraced

faith & nature as my guide, and cultivated trust in my inner voice.

The name Zenobia — my grandmother and mother’s name, meaning 'life of Zeus' — inspires me to bring depth, resilience, inner strength, and light to every meditative path I co-create.

Today, I help others craft mindful rituals that fit seamlessly into everyday life, fostering clarity, presence, and personal transformation.
